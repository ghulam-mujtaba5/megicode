## 2. LinkedIn Algorithm & SEO Audit

### How LinkedIn Search Actually Works in 2026
LinkedIn's search engine ranks company pages based on:

1. **Keyword density in About, Tagline, Specialties** — LinkedIn's internal search index scans these fields
2. **Posting frequency + engagement rate** — Active pages rank higher in search and feed
3. **Employee engagement** — When employees interact with company posts, reach multiplies 5-10x
4. **Follower growth velocity** — Steady growth signals relevance to the algorithm
5. **Profile completeness score** — LinkedIn gives priority to 100% complete pages
6. **External link signals** — Backlinks from your website/other platforms to LinkedIn improve discoverability

### Current Gaps Identified

| Gap | Current State | Optimized Target |
|-----|---------------|------------------|
| Tagline | Generic or not set | Keyword-rich, benefit-driven (120 chars) |
| About Section | Basic company description | SEO-optimized with keywords, CTAs, proof points |

## 7. Lead Generation Funnel via LinkedIn

### The Funnel Structure

```
AWARENESS → INTEREST → CONSIDERATION → ACTION
   ↓            ↓            ↓             ↓
Posts &     Case Studies   DM Outreach   Calendly
Thought     &Featured     & Connection   Booking
Leadership  Section       Requests      
```

### Stage 1: Awareness (Company Posts + Founder Posts)
- 3x/week company posts on thought leadership, case studies, team culture
- Both founders post 2-3x/week from personal profiles and tag @Megicode
- Use hashtags to appear in AI, startup, and software development feeds

### Stage 2: Interest (Featured Section + Social Proof)
When someone visits the profile after seeing a post:
- **Featured Section** shows CampusAxis (15,000 users = instant credibility)
- **About Section** clearly states services + results
- **Recommendations** from clients visible on founder profiles

### Stage 3: Consideration (DM Outreach + Connection Strategy)
- Connect with 10-20 startup founders per week (personalized note)
- Template: *"Hi [Name], I saw your work on [X] — impressive. We help founders like you move from idea to production with AI-powered software. Would love to connect."*
- Never pitch in the first message. Build rapport first.

### Stage 4: Action (Calendly CTA)
- Company page CTA button → calendly.com/megicode
- Every post ends with: "📅 Book a free call: calendly.com/megicode"
- Every DM conversation naturally flows to: *"Happy to jump on a quick 15-min call if you'd like to explore this further."*
